Flush

Flush all your washroom worries away with our Flush Unit!

Our Flush Unit uses enzymes to eliminate the source of malodors and deodorize your toilet bowl with every flush. It is easy to install and maintain. With every flush, you get clean, fresh, and bacteria-free toilet all the time. Perfect for home and business use.

Benefits

  1. When water is flush, water flows through the feed pipe into reservoir of dispenser.
  2. Eliminates the source of malodours and deodorizes with every flush.
  3. Living microbes produce enzymes to digest organic waste, scales and slime in pipeline to reduces blockages and damage to plumbing system.

Services Included

  1. Installation of the units.
  2. Weekly refill of Enzyme Solution.
  3. In case of breakdown, repair or replacement of unit is free of charge.

How it Works

  1. When water is flush, water flows through the feed pipe into reservoir of dispenser.
  2. As water surge upwards it creates air pressure in the special valve within the enzyme liquid container.
  3. When unit stop flushing, the water in the reservoir flows back into the feed pipe with a mixture of metered amount of enzyme.
