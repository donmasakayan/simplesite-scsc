Hand Dryer

Complete your hand wash experience with our automatic hand dryer machine.

Complete your customers’ washroom experience with our quality automatic hand dryer. Just place your hands within the machine’s sensor and dry your hands easily and stress-free.

SPECIFICATIONS:

Type: Automatic
Dimension(mm/in): 248(9 ¾) X 220(8 ¾) X 170(6 ¾)
Sound Level: 63 dB
Air Speed: 16.5 m/s---60Hz
Body: Chromed Cast Aluminum
Colors: White or Chrome
Warranty: One Year
Notes:
